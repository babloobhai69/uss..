It's you, Gemini Gurl

You belong to the Air element of the zodiac. Your main Gemini traits are being super-fast, super-smart, super-adaptable and super-curious people. All of this super-ness makes them fascinating to be around, albeit usually only for short spells because they can get easily distracted elsewhere and before you know it, they’re off. (Me being an exception)
Geminis are the uber-flirts of the zodiac, and can chat up anyone, anywhere, anytime. They never run out of news, info, gossip and charm, and they are ruled by Mercury - the planet of communication. Geminis always seem youthful, energetic, and full of fun and good humour.
Geminis are ruled by Mercury, the planet of travel and communication. Therefore, dates which feel like adventures, explorations, missions are absolute cat nip. You adore visiting, meeting, learning about and trying new things. You are a boundary-pusher and a curious observer of the world around you, and you need a partner who wants to do that too.
Clinginess. Oh my lord, it makes your teeth HURT when you feel like someone needs you around all of the time, pokes their nose into your every waking moment, and pours affection on you like custard on a sticky toff pud. Holy moly, it is so gross it gives you the instant ICK. Gemini, you need as independent and ~chilly~ a soul as your own to be truly compatible in love.
There is literally no “off” button. Bear in mind, older Gemmys may move from the night club into day drinking or dinner parties. The key is that whatever equates to partying in their mind is what they’ll prioritise above almost everything else on the schedule.
Geminis think fast, eat fast, move fast, talk fast, “get” stuff fast, change their mind fast… So much so that they can even meet themselves coming back round the other way! This can make them seem erratic, even duplicitous, because they don’t stick to their opinions or commitments. Tough shit.
Happily for them, they tend to be the Peter Pan of the zodiac - both physically and mentally. They are still into the latest trends long after everyone else has “settled down”.Geminis hate the idea of “settling down”, sounds dangerously like “dying” to them.The flip side of looking young and being so perky can mean they are also flaky, childish and silly at times. They also party WAY too much.
